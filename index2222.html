Why did I choose to come to RMIT?

There are 3 reasons why I chose to study in RMIT. The main first reason is because I want to live and work in Australia with my relatives in the future. Secondly, RMIT is a global university recognized for leadership and innovation in technology, design and enterprise. Therefore, studying in RMIT can give me best experience along with the IT skills to succeed in my career path. Finally, I can learn from IT experts working at the forefront of their fields and benefit from lecturers in RMIT.

What do I expect to learn during my studies?

Most people believe university is a big jump from high school, and for many it is an overwhelming change. For me, the first thing I expect when I study in university is adapting to the university culture so I can study more effectively. Moreover, I want to get more responsible for my study by doing projects and assignments so it will help me to prepare for professional working environment in the future. In addition, studying in RMIT is one of the best ways to develop my skills and meet new people from different culture. By working with them, I can gain new experiences that I may not otherwise have considered. It’s a great opportunity to explore and discover myself, developing both personally and professionally. About the IT program, I expect to gain all the necessary knowledge of programming to fulfill the requirements of IT jobs that every company need and I hope lecturers can help me to prolong my inspiration in this program.
